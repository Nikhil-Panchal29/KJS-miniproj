EXPERIMENT No 2:Laws Of Logic


INSTRUCTIONS:

1) Interface Overview:
a) The main window is divided into two sections:
i) Text Area (Center): This area will display the logical expressions corresponding to the selected logic law.
ii) Button Panel (Left): Contains buttons representing different logic laws.


2) Selecting a Logic Law:
a ) On the left side of the window, you will find a list of logic laws as buttons.
b) Click on a button representing the logic law you want to explore


3) Viewing Logical Expressions:
a) After clicking a button, the text area in the center will be updated with the logical expressions associated with the selected logic law.


4) Available Logic Laws:
a) The simulator includes buttons for the following logic laws:
i) Commutative Laws
ii) Associative Laws
iii) Distributive Laws
iv) Identity Laws
v) Negation Laws
vi) Idempotent Laws
vii) Null Laws
viii) Absorption Laws
ix) DeMorgan's Laws
x) Involution Laws


5) Exploring Different Laws:
a) Click on different law buttons to see how logical expressions change based on different laws.


6) Closing the Application:
a) Close the application window when you are done exploring by clicking the close button or closing the window


NOTE:
a) The logical expressions are displayed in a standard notation using symbols like ∧ (AND), ∨ (OR), ¬ (NOT), and ⇔ (if and only if).
b) The simulator is read-only, meaning you can view the logical expressions but cannot modify them.
